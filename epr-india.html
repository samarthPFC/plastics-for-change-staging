What is EPR

Extended Producer Responsibility,
simplified.

EPR credits in India require companies to ensure their packaging is recycled or properly handled. If not, they can buy credits from third parties handling the recycling and waste collection.

01

The Obligation

Companies that produce, import, or brand plastic packaging in India are required to ensure a proportion of that packaging is collected and recycled each year.

02

The Credit System

If a company cannot handle recycling directly, they can purchase EPR credits from authorized Plastic Waste Processors (PWPs) who collect and recycle on their behalf.

03

The Guarantee

The system guarantees all plastic waste receives proper processing regardless of origin. Every credit represents verified collection, recycling, and documentation.
