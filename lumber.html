Lumber for Change

A durable alternative to wood, made entirely from recycled plastic waste. Weather-resistant, termite-proof, and built to last with minimal maintenance.

Request Lumber Specs Why Choose Over Wood

Why choose over wood

Every advantage of wood,
none of the drawbacks.

Recycled plastic lumber outperforms traditional timber across every metric that matters.

01

Prevents Deforestation

Every unit of recycled plastic lumber directly reduces demand for timber, helping preserve forests and natural habitats.

02

Water & Pest Resistant

Immune to water damage, termites, and rot. No chemical treatments needed, unlike traditional wood that requires regular sealing and pest control.

03

Extended Lifespan

Outlasts traditional wood by years. Resistant to corrosion, moisture, and UV degradation, maintaining structural integrity over decades.

04

Minimal Maintenance

No painting, staining, or sealing required. Consistent dimensions and finish throughout the product's lifetime with zero upkeep costs.
