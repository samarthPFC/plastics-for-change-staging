Plastic Offsets

Offset Your Plastic Footprint With Verified Recovery

Certified plastic offset programs that help your brand neutralise its plastic footprint while creating verified social impact for waste collector communities across South and Southeast Asia.

Talk to an Expert How It Works

How it works

From commitment to
certified offset in 4 steps.

Our plastic offset program is designed to be simple for your brand and transformative for communities.

01

Define Your Target

Tell us your plastic footprint or voluntary offset goal. We design a recovery program that matches your exact volume and material type requirements.

02

Collection & Recovery

Our network of 12,000+ trained waste collectors recovers the agreed volume of plastic from the environment across India and Southeast Asia.

03

Processing & Verification

Recovered plastic is sorted, processed, and documented with full chain-of-custody traceability. Third-party audits verify every kilogram.

04

Certification & Reporting

You receive certificates of recovery, impact reports detailing collectors supported, and documentation ready for your sustainability reporting.

Why offset with us

Plastic offsets that deliver
real, verified impact.

Not all plastic offsets are equal. Ours are backed by the world's only Fair Trade certified collection network.

Fair Trade Certified

Every kilogram of plastic recovered through our program is collected by fairly paid waste collectors working in safe conditions. Your offset directly funds dignified livelihoods.

Social Impact Built In

Fair Trade premiums from your offset are invested directly into collector communities through housing, education, healthcare, and financial inclusion programs.

Third-Party Verified

Independent audits verify every kilogram. Full chain-of-custody documentation from collection point to processing facility ensures your offset claims stand up to scrutiny.
