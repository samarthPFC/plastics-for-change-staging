EPR compliance

Extended Producer Responsibility
solutions for India.

International brands selling into the Indian market face mandatory EPR obligations. We handle the entire compliance process.

EPR India Program

India's EPR regulations require brands to recover and recycle a proportion of the plastic packaging they place on the market. Our program handles the entire lifecycle, from collection through processing to certified documentation, so you stay compliant without building local infrastructure.

  • End-to-end EPR compliance management
  • CPCB registered and approved
  • Certified collection and recycling
  • Annual compliance reporting
  • No local infrastructure required
Discuss EPR Compliance

Voluntary Plastic Offsets

For brands without mandatory EPR obligations, our voluntary offset program lets you take proactive action on plastic pollution. Define your target volume, and we recover that amount through our certified collection network with full impact documentation.

  • Custom volume targets
  • Fair Trade verified recovery
  • Ocean Bound Plastic certified
  • Quarterly impact reporting
  • Brand storytelling content included
