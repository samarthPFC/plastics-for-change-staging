Fabric types

Four fabric categories for
every textile application.

From fashion apparel to technical textiles, every fabric is made from 100% post-consumer PET plastic.

Woven Fabrics

Recycled polyester woven textiles for apparel, furnishings, bags, and accessories. Available in a wide range of weights, weaves, and finishes.

  • Apparel and fashion
  • Home furnishings
  • Bags and accessories
  • Custom weave patterns
Get in Touch

Knit Fabrics

Recycled polyester knits engineered for activewear, casualwear, and performance apparel. Stretch, recovery, and moisture management built in.

  • Activewear and sportswear
  • Casualwear and t-shirts
  • Performance textiles
  • Custom GSM and stretch
Get in Touch

Non-Woven Fabrics

Recycled polyester non-wovens for linings, interlinings, technical applications, and industrial use. Consistent weight and strength.

  • Linings and interlinings
  • Technical applications
  • Filtration media
  • Industrial textiles
Get in Touch

Blended Fabrics

Recycled polyester blended with organic cotton, recycled nylon, or natural fibers. The best of both worlds for comfort and sustainability.

  • rPoly + organic cotton
  • rPoly + recycled nylon
  • rPoly + natural fibers
  • Custom blend ratios
