Certifications

Consumers want to support authentic sustainability and verified impact!

WFTO Certified

We're the first recycler verified by the World Fair Trade Organization, ensuring fair, transparent, and accountable practices that improve livelihoods for informal waste collectors.

B Corp

As a B Corp, we're part of a global community driving positive social and environmental impact - proof of our commitment to people and the planet.

OBP

As a certified Ocean Bound Plastic Collector, we're actively helping keep plastic out of our oceans and protecting marine ecosystems.

Social + OBP

We're the world's first collection organization certified under Social+OBP - proving our commitment to social responsibility and setting a new benchmark for sustainable plastic recycling.
