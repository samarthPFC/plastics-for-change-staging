Where your money goes

Three pillars of
lasting change.

Every donation is allocated across our three-pillar framework to create maximum, sustainable impact.

01

Economic & Social Empowerment

Dignified income, fair wages, safety equipment, and training for waste collectors. Financial inclusion through banking access and micro-insurance programs.

02

Community Development

Housing through the Namma Mane program, children's education, healthcare access, nutrition, rehabilitation services, and skill development.

03

Recycling Systems Development

Formalised waste management planning, collection infrastructure, technology for quality improvement, and 100% traceability systems.
