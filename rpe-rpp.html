Performance-driven applications

Engineered for the
toughest applications.

Our recycled polyolefins are designed to perform across a wide range of manufacturing applications.

01

Rigid Packaging

Bottles, caps, closures, and containers. Our rPE and rPP deliver the mechanical strength and process stability needed for high-speed packaging lines.

02

Automotive & Industrial

High-impact components, under-the-hood parts, industrial containers, and structural elements that require consistent density and tensile strength.

03

Household Goods

Durable consumer products, furniture components, storage containers, and everyday items manufactured to the same standards as virgin material.

04

Construction

Pipes, fittings, geomembranes, and building materials. Our rHDPE grades offer chemical resistance and long-term durability for infrastructure projects.

Quality assurance

control framework.
