Bricks for Change

Building materials that build more than structures. High-strength construction bricks made from recycled plastic waste, offering a sustainable alternative to traditional clay bricks.

Build With Impact See the Impact

Why recycled plastic bricks

Stronger foundations,
smaller footprint.

Our bricks convert low-value plastic waste into high-strength construction materials that outperform traditional alternatives.

01

High Strength

Engineered for structural integrity. Our recycled plastic bricks deliver comparable strength to traditional clay bricks while being lighter and easier to transport.

02

Weather Resistant

Built to withstand diverse environmental conditions. Resistant to water, humidity, and temperature fluctuations without cracking or degradation.

03

Reduces Clay Dependency

Every brick replaces traditional clay, reducing natural resource extraction and minimising the environmental impact of conventional brick manufacturing.

04

Circular Construction

Prevents plastic from reaching landfills and oceans while creating a new revenue stream for waste collectors in the recycling supply chain.
