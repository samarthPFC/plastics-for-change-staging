Our approach

Impact across two
interconnected pillars.

Our model creates lasting change by developing both individual stakeholders and entire communities.

Stakeholder Development

Empowering individual waste collectors with the tools, training, and infrastructure they need to build stable livelihoods.

  • Financial inclusion and banking access
  • Behavioural change and safety training
  • Fair Trade partner programs
  • Collection infrastructure development

  • Children's education
  • Rehabilitation services
  • Skill development and social inclusion

Featured programs

Programs that create
measurable change.

Our flagship initiatives directly improve the lives of waste collectors and their families.

Namma Mane Housing

Our housing initiative transforms low-value plastic waste into recycled planks used to build homes for migrant waste collector families. Namma Mane provides safe, dignified shelter that improves health outcomes, financial stability, and quality of life for some of the most vulnerable workers in our supply chain.

  • Homes built from recycled plastic planks
  • Safe shelter for migrant collector families
  • Improved health and financial outcomes
  • Scalable model across collection regions

E-Tricycle Program

Electric tricycles distributed to waste collectors in our supply chain reduce physical strain, increase collection capacity, and boost daily earnings. The program replaces manual pushcarts with clean electric vehicles, transforming how collectors work and live.

  • Electric vehicles for waste collectors
  • Reduced physical strain and injury
  • Increased collection capacity and earnings
  • Clean transport replacing manual pushcarts
