Material catalogue

one quality standard.

Every kilogram is traceable from individual waste collectors to your production line. Industrial-grade quality with built-in ESG compliance.

rPET Flakes & Granules

Ideal for packaging, bottles, and thermoformed products. Food-grade and non-food-grade resins with precise IV levels and low contamination rates.

  • Food-grade options available
  • IV 0.70-0.82 dl/g
  • Granules, flakes, and bales
  • FDA compliant
View rPET Details

Recycled PE & PP

Versatile recycled polyolefins for industrial and consumer applications. Durable resin engineered for injection moulding, extrusion, and rigid packaging.

  • rPP White, rHDPE White, rHDPE Grey
  • Consistent MFI per batch
  • Automotive and packaging grade
  • Verified density
View rPE & rPP Details

Recycled Polyester Fibers & Yarns

Circular inputs for sustainable textile manufacturing. High-performance fibers and yarns from 100% rPET feedstock for fashion and industrial textiles.

  • Spinning and filling fibers
  • Filament and melange yarns
  • GRS certified options
  • Custom denier and tenacity
