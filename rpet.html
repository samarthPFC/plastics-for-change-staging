Product portfolio

Three forms,
highest quality standards.

Every form of our rPET goes through the same rigorous quality process from collection to delivery.

rPET Granules

Production-ready pelletized rPET for direct injection moulding and extrusion. No recalibration required -drop-in replacement for virgin PET.

  • Food-grade options available
  • Precise IV levels (0.70–0.82 dl/g)
  • Low moisture content
  • Custom colour matching

rPET Flakes

Hot-washed, decontaminated PET flakes ready for compounding, sheet extrusion, and fibre spinning applications.

  • Hot and cold washed
  • Low PVC contamination
  • Consistent bulk density
  • Clear and coloured grades
Request Flake Specs

rPET Bales

Sorted, compressed post-consumer PET bottles in bale form for recyclers and processors who handle their own washing and flaking.

  • Colour-sorted (clear, blue, mixed)
  • Low foreign material
  • Consistent bale weight
  • Bulk volume pricing

Quality assurance

Supply chains you
can trust.

Every batch of rPET is tested, documented, and verified before it reaches your production line.

01

Precision Sorting

Advanced technology that removes contaminants at the source. Optical sorters and manual quality checks ensure only PET enters the processing line.

02

Decontamination

Multi-stage hot and cold washing for maximum purity. Our process removes labels, adhesives, and trace contaminants to meet food-contact standards.

03

Batch Integrity

Rigorous testing at every stage. IV measurement, moisture analysis, colour testing, and contamination checks before any shipment is released.

04

Verification

Full documentation for global regulatory compliance. Every batch comes with a Certificate of Analysis, chain-of-custody records, and ESG impact data.
